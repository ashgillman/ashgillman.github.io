Sprint Day – Oversized RC Cars

Apr 1st, 2014 12:00 am

The club held its first sprint day on Saturday. The sprint was held for the oversized RC car project, but also to test out the sprint day format as a possible standard format for club projects.

The aims of the day were to complete major aspects of the project: Android bluetooth communication code, Arduino bluetooth communication code, Arduino hardware interfacing, Relay replacement with MOSFETs, MOSFET H-bridge design

  • Android bluetooth communication code
  • Arduino bluetooth communication code
  • Arduino hardware interfacing
  • Relay replacement with MOSFETs
  • MOSFET H-bridge design, including PCB design (ready for milling)

In addition to the H-bridge circuitry, logic was implemented to ensure shorting 12V to GND was impossible. This also was able to reduce the required input pins to 2. Due to the higher current, it was decided to prototype the H-bridge using a terminal block. However, this monster ended up taking a lot of time and was very messy. I would recommend a better solution for future reference. Unfortunately, the MOSFET H-bridge design was not complete by the end of the day.
